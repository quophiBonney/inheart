ABOUT OUR INCORPORATION

PROVIDING SUPPORT AND LOVE.

Serves to empower, inspire, bring hope and support the community of 13 million widows in the United States and approximately 800,000 widowed annually. Most under the age of 55.

OUR VALUES

Compassion, partnership and love. Supporting our widows without judgement.

MISSION

To create a humane society by building a future for widowed people.

AIM

Build a community to support and inspire members after the death of a partner

WHO CAN YOU BE?

Collaboration

OUR COLLABORATION

In everything we do, we value perspectives, relationships, and participation. Helping widows through listening and evolving together as well as assiting and supporting one another for the sake of a common objective. It takes a village to raise a child.

VISION

OUR VISION

A community where the widow will have a purpose and live a fulfilled life while embracing their love for their departed spouse in an atmosphere of acceptance and support.

Integrity

OUR INTEGRITY

With the greatest amount of duty and accountability, serve. Making a consistent impression, not just via what we say but also through what we do, and doing so even when no one is looking.

TRENDING DEVELOPMENT

INSPIRING MILLION WIDOWS

We support widows through the grieving process and beyond by providing life transition resources and immediate connections. We facilitate the healing process which allows for rebuilding to start from our communities embrace by uplifting and encouraging widows to re-engage in life..

TRANSFORM A WIDOWS LIFE

Our vision is to be a strong network of widows empowering our members through shared experience. We open the door to a safe haven for widows, ensuring they do not go through their experience alone, but with life-long connections and lasting support. We relate. We understand. We listen. We care.

SAVING HOPELESS WIDOW

We value those natural individual differences such as culture, race, religion and disability that both challenge us and make each of us unique. Our programs focus on bringing out the best in each widow and respecting the unique values of staff, volunteers, partners, and the widows we serve.

DIFFERENCE WE ARE MAKING

Our existence for the past four years has been nothing but transformation and personal growth. We've lead many widows gain their hope and discover their true potentials in life so they can also contribute to the human race.
